About Mushavo

Property management should feel calm, clear, and under control.

Mushavo is a property operations platform for landlords, individual portfolio managers, and property management companies. It brings properties, units, tenants, leases, payments, proof files, maintenance, staff permissions, and subscriptions into one accountable workspace.

1

Clear record for each property, unit, tenant, and lease.

2

Controlled access for staff, IPMs, PMCs, and tenants.

3

Safer documents, payments, maintenance history, and audit trails.

Vision

Make property operations simpler across growing markets.

We want Mushavo to become a trusted operating system for property work in markets like Zimbabwe, Malaysia, and other countries where owners and managers need practical tools that fit local pricing and workflows.

Mission

Put every important property record in one clean place.

Mushavo helps teams manage rent, tenants, leases, maintenance, documents, staff permissions, and subscriptions with clear accountability, so records do not disappear when people move, change teams, or change landlords.

Goals

Build trust through reliable records and better workflows.

The goal is to reduce disputes, missed follow-ups, lost files, and unclear responsibility between landlords, tenants, staff, IPMs, and PMCs.

Who we serve

Built for the different people involved in property work.

Mushavo is not only for one type of property owner. It is designed for the full chain: owners, managers, company teams, staff, and tenants.

Landlords

Own and manage properties, tenants, leases, payments, staff, and maintenance.

Individual portfolio managers

Work for multiple landlords with approved access and clear limits.

Property management companies

Manage landlord relationships, company staff, permissions, and assigned portfolios.

Tenants and staff

Submit payments, report issues, upload files, and only see what they are allowed to access.

What guides us

Useful first. Beautiful because it is useful.

Mushavo should feel premium, but the design has to support the work. Every screen should make the next action obvious, protect records, and keep teams aligned.

Clarity

Important information should be easy to find and easy to understand.

Control

Owners decide who can view, edit, upload, approve, and manage.

Trust

Records should remain available even when accounts or relationships change.

Scale

Country pricing, subscriptions, IPMs, PMCs, and staff permissions are designed for growth.

Our product promise

Records should stay reliable even when people change.

When a tenant moves, a landlord changes manager, staff are removed, or a subscription pauses, the business history should remain understandable. Mushavo is being designed around long-term records, controlled deletion, clear archiving, and permission-based access.
