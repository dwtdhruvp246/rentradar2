About Mushavo

Practical software for property work that happens every day.

Mushavo exists to make property operations easier to run, easier to audit, and easier to share between the people who need access.

Vision

A property system where small landlords and larger operators can manage records, payments, maintenance, and teams without losing control of access.

Mission

Give landlords, tenants, IPMs, and PMCs a clear static-hosting friendly app backed by Supabase RLS, private storage, and precise permissions.

Goals

Keep tenant identities reusable, prevent duplicate lifecycle rows, protect files, separate rent from other money, and make plan limits understandable.

Values

Clarity, scoped access, honest records, customer-friendly errors, and operational screens that fit real phone and desktop use.

Mushavo. Your property, handled simply.
