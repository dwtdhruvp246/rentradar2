What Mushavo is

A practical command centre for property operations.

Instead of keeping records in chats, notebooks, spreadsheets, and separate folders, Mushavo keeps the important work connected to the right property, unit, tenant, landlord, staff member, and document.

For landlords

Create properties and units, invite tenants, log rent, upload lease PDFs, handle maintenance, and control what staff can see or change.

For individual portfolio managers

Work with multiple landlords, request access with a landlord code, and manage only the accounts and properties you are approved for.

For property management companies

Connect with landlords, assign company staff, manage permissions, and organize work across properties without mixing client records.

What it manages

The everyday property work that usually gets scattered.

Mushavo is designed around the real workflow: create the property, assign the unit, link the tenant, store the lease, track rent, handle issues, and keep every action accountable.

Properties and units

Track property details, unit status, occupancy, and vacancy in one place.

Tenants and leases

Payments and proof

Maintenance
